The "One Arm Band" Project

Winner: "Best Hackathon Overall App"

AT&T's IoT Hackathon in Boston

Woot, Woot! The "One Arm Band" project won 1st Place - $1,000 in Gift Cards for the team - at AT&T's 2015 Boston Hackathon!

We implemented and integrated a wide variety of technologies, including MediaTek Labs' LinkIt ONE. Here is what MediaTek Labs had to say:

Awesome! We really liked your project! Congratulations on winning "Best IoT App Overall" for One Arm Band. We would like to thank you for giving the LinkIt ONE a try and we were impressed how you got all the technology to work together. Well done! :-)
